I am proud of you

Truly.

Not because of what we were.
But because of who you are becoming.

I am deeply proud of the woman you have become — professionally, personally, entirely.
Your progress. Your discipline. Your evolution.
It shows. And it is beautiful.

There are many things we both could have handled better.
We were imperfect. Emotional. Learning.
But we did what we could with what we had at the time.
Not to justify it — just to acknowledge it.

I would have loved to be there in this chapter of your life,
to watch you rise even higher.
To grow alongside you.
But I am not strong enough to stand beside you
and watch you build it with someone else.
Not again.

And no, I am not angry.
Well… maybe a little.
Sometimes my thoughts consume me and I replay everything.
But even then, I choose to wish you well.

Maybe we had that In-yeon —
that connection written somewhere beyond us.
But maybe we have not yet lived through the eight thousand layers
that would allow us to meet again in another lifetime and stay.

I did not think it would be this complicated in the 21st century.
Yet here we are.
Right now, I feel a little like Evelyn watching Celia on television.

I hope you liked the flowers I left at your door —
the same door I watched you open so many times:
with happiness, with sadness, with anger. Anyways, I am not here to talk about what I feel.

You still have so many skies to conquer.
And as I once told you,
I will always pray for you in every flight you take.

I truly hope one day I get to see you fly —
or maybe even be on a flight with you.

No expectation.
Just gratitude.
